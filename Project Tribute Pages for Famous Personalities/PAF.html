PAK AIR FORCE


(A SYMBOL OF PRIDE FOR THE NATION)

The Pakistan Air Force seeks to defend its nation and its interests, whilst preserving its freedoms, through the use of technology, willpower and working side by side.


OUR BRAVE HEROES

Squadron leader Hassan Siddiqui

Hassan Siddiqui is a lionhearted pilot of Pakistan Air force who made millions of Pakistanis proud by spilling the 2 Indian jets and saved the country from a big loss in 2019.

Flying Officer Marium Mukhtiar Shaheed

Female Pilot Marium Mukhtiar was the first female Pakistani fighter pilot to be killed in the line of duty.She died flying a PAF FT-7PG aircraft that crashed near Kundian in Mianwali District

Pilot Officer Rashid Minhas

Rashid Minhas was the only PAF officer to receive the highest valour award, the Nishan-e-Haider. He was also the youngest person and the shortest-serving officer to have received this award.

Air Commodore Muhammad Mahmood Alam

MM Alam was a Pakistani fighter pilot officially credited by the Pakistan Air Force for having downed five Indian Air Force Hawker Hunter aircraft in 1 minute during the Indo-Pakistani War of 1965 on 7 September 1965.

Wing Commandor Nouman Akram Shaheed

Wing Commander Noman Akram, a career officer of Pakistan Air Force (PAF), embraced Shahadat when his F-16 aircraft crashed near the Shakarparian area of the federal capital while carrying out rehearsal for the Pakistan Day Parade.

LIEUTENANT GENERAL NIGAR JOHAR

Major General Nigar Johar is three-star general in Pakistan Army. Nigar is the first and only woman in the history of Pakistan Army to reach the rank of lieutenant general, and third to reach the rank of major general.

Squadron Leader Saira Amin

Saira Amin is the First Female Fightor Pilot. Squadron Leader saira amin is also the first female PAF officer to win sword of honour at the passing-out parade of the 117th GD pilot course.

Squadron Leader Tanveer Shaheed

Squadron Leader Tanveer Shaheed from the 'Bandits' Squadron who was martyred as his Mirage aircraft crashed in Karachi during Operational Night Training on November 21st, 2014.
