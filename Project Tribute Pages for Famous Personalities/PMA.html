CAPTAIN ASFAND YAR BUKHARI SHAHEED

Our Proud

CAPTAIN ROOHULLAH SHAHEED

Shahadat 24 Oct 2016, while stopping a suicide bomber during hostage rescue operation at Police Training Centre Quetta. He was performing duty as an anti-terrorism platoon commander at that time.

LIEUTENANT GENERAL NIGAR JOHAR

Major General Nigar Johar is three-star general in Pakistan Army. Nigar is the first and only woman in the history of Pakistan Army to reach the rank of lieutenant general, and third to reach the rank of major general.

CAPTAIN NAWABZAD JAZIB REHMAN SHAHEED

3rd February 2018,he embraced martyrdom in Swat, Kabal in a suicide attack.Capt Jazib Shaheed showcased his fearlessness then and there by attempting to entangle with the terrorist.

CAPTAIN ABDUL QADEER BALOCH SHAHEED SHAHEED

He died on June 2, 2018, while his car was on way to Khuzdar from Quetta. A female relative sitting on the passenger seat sustained injuries in the accident in Lakoran area, while Qadeer Ahmed died on the spot.
